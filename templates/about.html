About Aptify

Our Mission

Aptify helps BTech students prepare for placement exams with dynamic quizzes and detailed analytics. We believe in providing a focused, distraction-free environment for practicing aptitude questions that are commonly asked in campus placement tests.

Features

  • Dynamic Question Generation

    AI-powered MCQ generation across various aptitude categories using the Google Gemini API.

  • Performance Analytics

    Detailed score breakdowns, question-by-question analysis, and visual charts.

  • PDF Reports

    Download comprehensive PDF reports with performance metrics and charts.

  • Leaderboard

    Track your progress and compare with peers (no login required).

Future Roadmap

Custom Difficulty Levels

Choose between easy, medium, and hard questions based on your preparation level.

Offline Mode

Practice even without an internet connection with preloaded question sets.

Company-Specific Question Banks

Practice with questions tailored to specific company placement exams.
